De-Fi for Frisbee

$FRISBEE will migrate to the low-cost, PoS (Proof-of-Stake) L2 Shibarium network.

How do I make passive-income?

You can earn passive-income by staking your $FRISBEE tokens for a set time and recieve an interest.
Another avenue would be to stake LP tokens, so you will choose the desirbed pool (for example $FRISBEE/$USDT) and provide liquidity to that pool which would then give you LP tokens. You then stake these LP tokens in the corresponding pool and earn native Shibarium tokens.

Download either 'MetaMask' or 'TrustWallet', purchase some Ethereum then head over to either Uniswap or Shibswap.
Find the CA shown in the last question, and paste it into the swap so Ethereum is being swapped for $FRISBEE, set your slippage and swap!
